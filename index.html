The Gathering Resonance Collective(GRC) is made up of community members who provide space and need-based services for learning, connecting and creating. We are located upstairs, in The Pink House (2474 N. Booth). Our space is available to rent*: for classes, workshops, meetings, ceremonies, gatherings and performances. We have open work or study hours. Check our calendar for open hours and events. Connect with us at 920-238-3948 or via Telegram and connect to GRC_Jess.

*rental of space is covered by a suggested donation. GRC can host your next event, provide childcare and/or facilitate discussions for an additional donation (for more info or to schedule, contact us at gatheringresonance@gmail.com)
